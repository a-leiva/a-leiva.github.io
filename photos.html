Life in Stills

A collection of still images I've taken so far. Click to view larger.

Wesley vigil for FSU - Students and members of Gator Wesley Foundation on West University Avenue hold hands and pray Thursday afternoon. The prayer vigil was held in response to the Florida State University shooting Thursday morning.

Treon under investigation - A University Police Department officer, left, and a Gainesville Police public service technician transport evidence bags to a GPD forensic crime unit vehicle at the Springs Residential Complex at about 2:30 p.m. Monday. The residence hall is the location of an alleged sexual assault that took place Sunday morning.

Paws on Parole - Officer Debra Scott of the Alachua County Jail hugs Maggie, a 10-month-old dog, at the Paws on Parole Unleashed program graduation Monday morning. The program pairs adoptable dogs with inmates who train them for eight weeks to pass the American Kennel Club’s Canine Good Citizen test.

Scary Good Makeup - Penny DiPalma, a UF staff member at the UF Teaching Center, applies makeup at the zombie makeup workshop in the Reitz Union Grand Ballroom on Wednesday evening.

Homecoming 2014 - A member of the Gainesville chapter of the Korean War Veterans Association marches as part of the Homecoming Parade.

Kent and Linda Fuchs - Kent Fuchs and his wife, Linda, Gator Chomp after the Cornell University provost was named UF’s 12th president at Emerson Alumni Hall on Wednesday.
